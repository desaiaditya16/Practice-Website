Overview of MurPHy's Design

Shooter

For our shooter, we used a cam and follower with surgical tubing to provide consistent elastic energy. This way, even when the battery is running low, there is a consistent shot.

Lift

We decided to use a curved, circular lifter to maximize the surface area that is in contact with the cap ball.
